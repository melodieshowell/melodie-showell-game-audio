Wwise Combat VO System

Video walkthrough of combat system, running via a custom WAAPI controller. Recorded by amateur actors from home studios, directed, edited and implemented by me.

[ Video embed goes here — swap for your Vimeo iframe ]
  • Human / Werewolf branching — Switch Container gating two fully independent effort-sound trees from a single Event.
  • HP × Attack Level — Blend Container bands driven by Player_HP, each containing a Switch Container for discrete Light/Heavy selection, avoiding crossfaded VO layering.
  • Hit cancels taunt — a single Event carries two Actions, Play the hit reaction and Stop the enemy taunt, so a landed hit always cuts the line cleanly.
  • Ducking — player dialogue ducks the combat effort bus automatically, so inner-voice lines stay legible over unpredictable combat noise.
  • WAAPI controller — a small Python/Tkinter tool that drives the whole system externally, Events, RTPCs, and Switches, simulating what a game engine would send.
  • Coming soon — dialogue post-processing states and a Reaper → Wwise loudness-matching pass, in progress.
